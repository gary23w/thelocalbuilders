Who we are

A renovation crew your neighbours already trust

We’re a family-run shop that’s been fixing up and building out homes across the Greater Toronto Area since 2004. In that time we’ve wrapped up more than 500 jobs, and most of our new work still walks in the door because a past customer told a friend about us.

Big or small, you get the same thing: a crew that shows up when it says it will, does the work properly, and gives you a straight answer on price before anyone picks up a tool.

Good to know

Questions we hear a lot

How much does a renovation cost?

Every job is different, so we don’t guess. Tell us what you need and we’ll come look, then give you a written quote with the price spelled out. The quote is free and there’s no obligation.

What areas do you cover?

We cover the Greater Toronto Area and the wider region within about 200 km of Toronto — from Niagara and Hamilton to Kitchener-Waterloo, Barrie and Muskoka, Peterborough, Belleville and Prince Edward County. Not sure if you’re in range? Just ask.

Do you take on small jobs, or only big renovations?

Both. A leaky eavestrough, a few steps of concrete, a single room — or a full interior and exterior overhaul. No job is too small.

How fast can you get back to me?

Usually the same day. Call 647-507-8853 and a real person picks up, or send the form and we’ll follow up quickly.

Do you handle commercial work?

Yes. We do both residential and commercial projects across the region, from homes to storefronts and small business spaces.
